## Tool Selection Guidelines

### For Quick Lookups
- People: LinkedIn, Hunter.io, Namechk
- Companies: OpenCorporates, Crunchbase, BuiltWith
- Domains: crt.sh, DNSDumpster, VirusTotal

### For Deep Investigation
- People: PACER, Pipl, full social media audit
- Companies: SEC EDGAR, PitchBook, DomainTools
- Threats: Shodan, Maltego, full threat intel stack

### Cost-Effective Stack (Free Tools)
1. OpenCorporates (company data)
2. crt.sh + DNSDumpster (domain intel)
3. LinkedIn (professional)
4. VirusTotal (threat analysis)
5. Wayback Machine (historical)
6. Shodan (limited free)
7. AbuseIPDB (IP reputation)

### Enterprise Stack (Paid Tools)
1. DomainTools (comprehensive WHOIS)
2. SecurityTrails (DNS intelligence)
3. ZoomInfo (B2B data)
4. Maltego (link analysis)
5. Shodan Enterprise (full access)
6. PitchBook (investment data)
